Shinkansen

The Shinkansen (a.k.a. Bullet Train) is a network of high-speed railway lines in running throughout Japan. While it may not be the fastest way to travel in Japan, it is the most convenient and comfortable way to travel. The Shinkansen is most known for its high-speed trains that can reach speeds of up to 320 km/h (200 mph).
